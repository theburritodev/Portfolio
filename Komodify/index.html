What is Komodify?

Komodify is a free project manager that all your projects are saved in a local storage.

Why use Komodify?

About us

Komodify is a project manager that saves your projects directly to the website's local storage, ensuring security and privacy without the need for external servers. Our platform allows you to organize your tasks in a simple and efficient way, with an intuitive interface that makes navigation and use easy. Furthermore, we share projects in a practical way through unique links, allowing for easy and quick collaboration. Komodify is ideal for students, professionals and anyone who wants to keep their projects organized and accessible.
